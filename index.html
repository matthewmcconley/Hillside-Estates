Our Mission & Vision

Our Mission

Hillside Estates at Franklin is a well-maintained manufactured housing community located on 63 acres in Franklin, Sussex County, New Jersey. We are committed to providing a safe, clean, and welcoming environment for our residents.

Our Vision

Originally established in 1958, the community has evolved over decades into what is now Hillside Estates at Franklin, with continued investment in infrastructure and long-term sustainability.

Our Community

Set in the Woods of Franklin

Hillside Estates at Franklin is tucked into the wooded hills of Sussex County. Quiet, tree-lined streets — Skyview, Woodbine, Zimmer, Cedar, Alpine Circle, and Sunrise — wind through an established community of more than 190 homes, just off Munsonhurst Road.
