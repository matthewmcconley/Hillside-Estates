Our Mission & Vision

Our Mission

Hillside Estates at Franklin is a well-maintained manufactured housing community located on 63 acres in Franklin, Sussex County, New Jersey. We are committed to providing a safe, clean, and welcoming environment for residents.

Our Vision

Originally established in 1958, the community has evolved over decades into what is now Hillside Estates at Franklin, with continued investment in infrastructure and long-term sustainability.

Rules & Regulations

Garbage Management

All garbage must be placed in trash receptacles and kept in your shed until pickup day.

Quiet and Peaceful Enjoyment

Residents are expected to be considerate of neighbors and help maintain the quiet character of the community.

Maintenance Standards

Homes, lots, decks, steps, and outdoor areas should be kept clean, orderly, and in good repair.

Vehicles & Parking

Vehicles should be parked only in approved areas and must follow all posted and community parking guidelines.

Guests

Residents are responsible for ensuring guests follow community standards while visiting Hillside Estates.

Questions about a rule?

Contact the office if you need help understanding community guidelines or need a copy of current documents.
